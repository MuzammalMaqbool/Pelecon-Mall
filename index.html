Who We Are

About Pelican Builders

Pioneering the future of real estate development in Pakistan

Building Dreams Since 2015

Pelican Builders stands as one of Pakistan's most forward-thinking real estate development and services groups. Since our inception in 2015, we have been an integral part of the DHA support team, playing a crucial role in the development of DHA Bahawalpur under the visionary leadership of our CEO, Mr. Malik Zulfiqar Ahmad.

Our commitment extends beyond construction – we create communities, shape lifestyles, and build legacies. Pelican Builders exclusively offers luxury properties in DHA Bahawalpur, featuring the prestigious Pelican Mall and Swiss International Hotels and Resorts.

With a focus on detail-driven developments, we ensure that every project reflects our core values of quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Our portfolio speaks volumes about our dedication to transforming the real estate landscape of Bahawalpur.
